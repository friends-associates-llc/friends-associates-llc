Mr. Shankar P. Bansal

With over five decades of experience in the global fine jewellery industry, Mr. Shankar P. Bansal is the visionary behind Friends Associates LLC. His career is marked by an unwavering dedication to artisanal craftsmanship, business integrity, and cultural legacy.

From his formative years in the traditional gemstone markets of India to building one of the most trusted global jewellery trade networks, Mr. Bansal has always led with heart, humility, and unmatched expertise.

Today, he remains not only a pioneer but also a mentor—empowering artisans, championing ethical sourcing, and shaping the future of fine jewellery with purpose and pride.

“Jewellery is not just adornment — it’s a cultural language of celebration, identity, and timeless beauty.”
