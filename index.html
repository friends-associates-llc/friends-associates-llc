Who We Are

At Friends Associates LLC, we specialize in the international trade of meticulously handcrafted gold and diamond jewelry. With a legacy rooted in artisanal excellence and global trust, we strive to deliver unmatched craftsmanship and timeless beauty to clients across the world.

What We Do

We import and export a curated collection of handmade gold and diamond jewelry. Each piece reflects exquisite attention to detail and cultural heritage, blending traditional techniques with contemporary elegance. We work closely with designers, artisans, and global partners to offer:

  • Custom bridal and ceremonial pieces
  • Fine jewellery for international markets
  • Wholesale and retail partnerships
  • Trusted sourcing and export logistics

Meet the Founder

Mr. Shankar P. Bansal brings over 50 years of experience in the global jewellery industry. A pioneer in handmade designs, Mr. Bansal's vision continues to shape our dedication to quality, trust, and elegance in every product we offer.
