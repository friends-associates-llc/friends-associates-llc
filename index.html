Welcome to Friends Associates

We are a global trade company specializing in the import and export of handcrafted gold and diamond jewellery. With decades of excellence and heritage, our work embodies sophistication, legacy, and trust.

“Our jewellery is more than a product — it's a piece of culture, a story of skill, and a promise of integrity.”

Our Core Values

  • Heritage: Honoring traditions through timeless designs.
  • Excellence: Uncompromising quality in every piece we export.
  • Integrity: Ethical sourcing and fair trade practices globally.
  • Innovation: Blending classic craftsmanship with modern design.

Craftsmanship & Global Presence

At Friends Associates, our artisans are the heart of what we do. Each piece is handcrafted with care, precision, and a deep respect for the centuries-old traditions of jewellery-making.

With established trade channels across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East, we bring our unique creations to clients who seek not just luxury — but meaning. Our company has proudly built relationships with independent boutiques, high-end retailers, and legacy jewelers around the world.
